# 🌱 Renewable Energy Powered IoT System for Plant Water Stress Monitoring

## 📖 Overview

Agriculture consumes a significant portion of global freshwater resources, and conventional irrigation methods often lead to overwatering or under-watering. This project presents a Renewable Energy Powered IoT System for Plant Water Stress Monitoring that directly measures plant water stress using stem impedance sensing and automatically controls irrigation.

The system integrates renewable energy sources, including Solar Photovoltaic (PV) and Plant Microbial Fuel Cell (PMFC), to achieve sustainable and energy-efficient operation. Real-time monitoring and visualization are enabled through IoT connectivity using ESP8266 and ThingSpeak.

## 🔄 System Workflow

1. Sensors collect environmental and plant stress data.
2. Stem impedance is measured to determine plant water stress.
3. Data is transmitted to the cloud using ESP8266.
4. ThingSpeak visualizes real-time sensor readings.
5. Irrigation pump is automatically activated when stress exceeds the threshold.
6. Solar PV and PMFC provide sustainable power to the system.
